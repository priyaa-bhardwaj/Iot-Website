Internet of Things(IoT)

The Beginning


The actual term “Internet of Things” was coined by

Kevin Ashton in 1999,

during his work at Procter&Gamble. Ashton who was working in supply chain optimization.
Because the internet was the hottest new trend in 1999 and because it somehow made sense, he called his presentation

“Internet of Things”.

The term Internet of Things did not get widespread attention for the next 10 years.
